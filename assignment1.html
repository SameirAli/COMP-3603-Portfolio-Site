Deliverable 1

Ideas:

  1. Universal Time Converter App - Allows the user to easily convert the time from one time zone to another.

  2. Healthy Meal Prep app - The user is able to choose different ingredients and create a meal. Once the meal is created, the app will tell the user the amount of calories and the nutritional content the meal contains. The app makes suggestions to the user on how to make their meals healthier.

  3. Exercise Timer App - The user is able to create different workout timers. These timers will take into account the amount of time for the reps, sets, and rest. The user will be able to save the timers for different workouts as profiles and may access them using the app, e.g. Chest Workout Profile, Leg Workout Profile.

Deliverable 2

After some consideration, the Healthy Meal Prep app was chosen as the most promising idea. The Healthy Meal Prep app is useful to a large amount of people compared to the other two. The Healthy Meal Prep app is able to be used by individuals who simply want to diet. These individuals maybe into exercising but do not have to be. The Exercise Timer App is limited to only people who are interested in exercising. The Universal Timer Converter app is very basic in comparison to the Healthy Meal Prep App. The universal time converter app may be used once or twice a year by some individual, however if someone is dieting then they will be constantly using the Healthy Meal Prep App.

Deliverable 4

The app is intended to assist people who are on a diet, planning to go on a diet, or wish to eat healthier in general. These people can range from teenagers to the elderly, the app is meant to be able to assist anyone in creating healthier meals.

Deliverable 5

Scenarios:

  1. Timmy is a teenage boy who has been gaining weight. He decides to go on a diet in order to lose the extra pounds. Timmy uses the Healthy Meal Prep app in order to make dieting easier. Through the use of this app, Timmy is able to make healthy low calorie meals and is able to get back to a healthy weight.
  2. Johnny is a new fitness instructor and hasn't learned a great amount about meal prep yet. He understands the amount of calories and nutrients necessary for muscle growth. Johnny uses the Healthy Meal prep app to create many meals that fit his nutritional and calorie needs. Johnny is able to share these meals with his clients.
  3. George is a 90 year old man who has been diagnosed with diabetes. His doctor has given him a specific dietary plan to ensure George is able to stay healthy and manage his diabetes. George uses the Healthy meal prep app to plan his meals in accordance to his doctors guidelines. The app allows him to meet his nutrient and caloric needs.

Deliverable 6

The Healthy Meal Prep app will allow the user to enter the ingredients of their meal. The app will display the amount of calories contained in the meal, as well as the nutritional content of the meal. The app will give suggestions to the user, based on the ingredients, on how to reduce the calories of the meal and increase the nutrients of the meal. The user will be able to save recipes for meals and view it later. The app will feature accounts for the user. On first use of the app, the user will have to register and make an account. The user can then login and view previously made recipes, as well as save new recipes. In additions, users will be able to share the healthy recipes they made on social media. The main social media platforms that users will be allowed to share on are Facebook, Instagram, Discord and Whatsapp. The account data for the users will be stored on cloud servers. These servers will be rented and depending on the success of the app, some private servers may be made to better facilitate the users. These servers can be rented from providers such as Amazon.
